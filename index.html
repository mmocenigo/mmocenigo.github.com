Marissa A. Mocenigo

Computer Scientist, Fine Dining Enthusiast

Marissa is a passionate and dedicated computer scientist. She completed her undergraduate degree at Bryn Mawr College in May 2011 and started her career at MIT Lincoln Laboratory after graduation. At MIT LL, she played an integral role in the design and development of a radar software suite to organize and analyze incoming data. Marissa left MIT LL in 2013 to pursue a UI software engineering position at Rethink Robotics. Her development work focused on front-end, user-interface design using C++ with QT.

In 2014, she joined Carbonite with the intent of greatly expanding and developing her skillset to other technologies and languages. Today, she works on a cloud backup project using Python, AngularJS, and C#. Her research interests include human-computer interaction, UI design, and data visualization.

In her spare time, she can be found exploring new movies and local cuisine.
