Marissa A. Mocenigo

Computer Scientist, Fine Dining Enthusiast

Marissa is a passionate and dedicated computer scientist. She completed her undergraduate degree at Bryn Mawr College in May 2011 and now works full-time at MIT Lincoln Laboratory. Here, she is helping to develop a radar software suite to organize and analyze incoming data. Her development work focuses on front-end, user-interface design using C++ with QT. Her research interests include human-computer interaction, UI design, and data visualization.

In her spare time, she can be found exploring new movies and local cuisine.
